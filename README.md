# tableau-healthcare_dashboard
Tableau dashboard analyzing healthcare datasets to uncover insights on billing costs, patients demographics, and admission types.

## Tool Used
Tableau Public

## Dataset
Healthcare Dataset (Kaggle) - simulated hospital records including patient demographics, admission details, billings, and insuarance information.

## Key Insights
 1. Patients diagnosed with obesity had the highest average billing amount (approximately $25.6K), although the differences across medical conditions were relatively small. 

2. The numbers of elective, emergency, and urgent admissions were distributed fairly evenly, suggesting a balanced pattern of hospital admissions in this dataset. 

3. The patient population was almost evenly divided between male and female, indicating no noticeable gender imbalance. 

4. Average billing amounts were similar across insurance providers, suggesting limited variation in treatment costs among insurers.
